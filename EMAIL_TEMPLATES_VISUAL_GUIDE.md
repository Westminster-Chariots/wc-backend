## Responsive Behavior

### Desktop (600px)
- Full width cards with padding
- Two-column layouts where applicable
- Large buttons with generous padding

### Mobile (< 600px)
- Single column layout
- Stacked elements
- Full-width buttons
- Reduced padding for better fit

---

## Email Client Compatibility

✅ **Tested and Compatible**:
- Gmail (Web, iOS, Android)
- Apple Mail (macOS, iOS)
- Outlook (Web, Desktop)
- Yahoo Mail
- ProtonMail

⚠️ **Known Limitations**:
- Some older email clients may not support gradients (fallback to solid color)
- Emoji support varies by client (fallback to text labels)

---

## Accessibility Features

- ✅ Semantic HTML structure
- ✅ High contrast text (WCAG AA compliant)
- ✅ Clear visual hierarchy
- ✅ Descriptive link text
- ✅ Alt text for icons (where applicable)
- ✅ Readable font sizes (minimum 14px)

---

## Performance

- **Email Size**: ~15-20KB (HTML only)
- **Load Time**: Instant (no external resources)
- **Images**: None (uses Unicode symbols and HTML/CSS)
- **Fonts**: System fonts (no web fonts)

---

## A/B Testing Recommendations

### Test Variations:
1. **CTA Button Color**: Blue vs. Green
2. **Header Style**: Gradient vs. Solid
3. **Icon Style**: Unicode vs. Emoji vs. None
4. **Content Length**: Detailed vs. Concise
5. **Urgency Indicators**: Badge vs. Text vs. None

### Metrics to Track:
- Open rate
- Click-through rate (CTA button)
- Time to payment completion
- Customer support inquiries
